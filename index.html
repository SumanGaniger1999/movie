AVANE SRIMANNARAYANA

CAST:

DIRECTORS: Sachin Ravi WRITER: Ganesh Hegde

MUSIC BY: Ajaneesh PRODUCER: H.K.Prakash

STORYLINE

Story of Corrupt Cop who is intelligent and brighter than normal people.The character is said to have the features of Sherlock holmes and Jack Sparrow combined.The story runs through the place called Amaravathi.This is a period RomCom with plenty of Action Sequences.Its a fictional
story taking place in a world created by 7 Writers including Rakshith Shetty.
